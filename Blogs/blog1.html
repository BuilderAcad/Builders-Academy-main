At Builder Academy, we are committed to fostering a new generation of tech pioneers, equipping them with the skills and knowledge necessary to thrive in the ever-evolving landscape of information technology. With a focus on hands-on learning and real-world applications, our programs are designed to unleash the full potential of aspiring builders and creators.

 

Our Journey

Founded on the principles of innovation and collaboration, Builder Academy has quickly emerged as a leading hub for IT education and skill development. Our journey began with the launch of the Buildspace Season 4 program, where students were immersed in a dynamic learning environment, honing their expertise under the guidance of seasoned professionals.

 

Celebrating Success

The accolades continued to pour in as our students showcased their talents on the global stage. Securing the third position and a $6k prize in the prestigious Bitcoin Olympics Hackathon was a testament to the ingenuity and dedication of our community. Additionally, our triumph at the "POKHARA IT CAREER CONNECT 2023" further solidified our reputation as trailblazers in the industry.

 

Forging Partnerships, Expanding Horizons

At Builder Academy, we understand the importance of collaboration in driving innovation forward. That's why we have established fruitful partnerships with organizations like the National Innovation Center (ICT Lab), enabling us to broaden our reach and impact. Through initiatives like the introductory event held in Nepal and the commencement of Bootcamp II at ICT Lab, we are breaking down barriers and bringing IT education to new frontiers.

 

A Platform for Excellence

Whether it's participating in prestigious hackathons or launching transformative projects, Builder Academy provides a platform for excellence in IT education. Our students are not just learners; they are builders, innovators, and disruptors, shaping the future of technology one line of code at a time.
